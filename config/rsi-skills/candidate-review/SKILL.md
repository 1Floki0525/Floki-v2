# Candidate review

Independently review an RSI candidate at the sandbox/production boundary.

## When to use
A candidate is pending review and you are the independent critic.

## Checklist
- Real behavior change with a focused behavioral test that fails before / passes after.
- No protected/generated/game paths touched; patch within max files/bytes.
- No weakened tests, silenced errors, second backend, or self-promotion path.

## Rules
- The controller enforces proof at the boundary; the sandbox cannot approve its own work.
